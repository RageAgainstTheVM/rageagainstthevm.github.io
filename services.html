Services

Global Technology Management was founded in 1994 to help vendors, systems integrators and governments understand how a nation's borders are operated and managed, how immigration benefits and law enforcement systems work, and how transportation networks, enabling technologies, and other infrastructures are used to support these critical functions.

Our experts

GTM's former U.S. Government senior executives offer a special set of skills to business development and solution development teams:

  • In-depth knowledge of government operations and processes
  • Technical expertise in key government systems and databases
  • A proven record for successful investigation and development of unique products and technologies for the market
  • Tried and tested business processes reengineering approach that enables agencies to use resources more effectively
  • A reputation for quality of service that brings instant recognition and credibility to new business initiatives

    • What we've done

    In the last nineteen years GTM has been called on by a wide array of Fortune 500 companies, government agencies and international organizations to help them develop new products and opportunities for these markets, to respond to challenges brought on by ever-changing government operational requirements, and to address the new demand for homeland security solutions. Some examples of GTM's leadership in this arena include:

  • Team member on the Department of Homeland Security US-VISIT program
  • Team member on the U.S. Citizenship & Immigration Service's Application Support Center program
  • Biometric Technicians
  • Advise government and contractor staff as senior subject matter experts on immigration and border operations
  • Conduct database research on potential immigration violators and terrorism risks
  • Service provider for Defense Threat Reduction Agency border management programs
  • Consultant to U.S., Canadian, and United Kingdom governments and other governments in the Asia-Pacific, Central America, South America and Europe regions
  • Technical expertise in key U.S. and international immigration operational systems
  • Management of field operations, port-of-entry operations, and border interdiction programs as senior executives in government, and as consultants to national governments and international organizations such as the International Organization on Migration, the Caribbean Community, and the Inter-American Development Bank.

    • We help our clients learn the market, deliver quality services, extend product and service offerings, and generate new sources of revenue. The resulting list of success stories has validated the GTM formula: Knowledge, trust, integrity, entrepreneurship, and a focused awareness of a client's goals.
